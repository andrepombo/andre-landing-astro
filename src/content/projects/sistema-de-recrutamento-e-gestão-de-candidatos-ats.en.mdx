This custom ATS centralizes the entire hiring process in one place: candidate profiles, pipeline management, interview scheduling, analytics and reports — all inside a productivity‑focused internal dashboard for HR teams.

---

## Project overview

Goals for this build:

* Detailed candidate records and search
* Visual recruitment pipeline with Kanban view
* Integrated interview calendar and reminders
* WhatsApp notifications (Z‑API)
* Analytics, demographics and insights modules
* Custom questionnaires and candidate scoring
* Activity logs and PDF/Excel exports

---

## Stack

* Backend: Django + Django REST Framework, JWT auth
* Frontend: React + TypeScript, TailwindCSS
